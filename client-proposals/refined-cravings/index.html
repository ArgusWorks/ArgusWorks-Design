Refined Cravings Story

Since 2016, we've been elevating events across the Inland Empire, Orange County, and Los Angeles with our signature approach to charcuterie artistry.

Our journey began with a passion for bringing people together over beautifully crafted food experiences. What started as intimate gatherings has grown into a premier catering service trusted by couples, corporations, and event planners throughout Southern California.

In 2020, we expanded our offerings to include contactless delivery and "board-to-door" services, ensuring that exceptional taste and presentation remain accessible for every occasion.
