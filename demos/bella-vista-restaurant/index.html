About Bella Vista

Since 1995, Bella Vista has been bringing authentic Italian flavors to Seattle. Our family recipes, passed down through four generations, celebrate the rich culinary traditions of Tuscany and Sicily.

Chef Marco Rossini leads our kitchen with over 20 years of experience, using only the finest imported ingredients alongside fresh local produce. Every dish is prepared with passion and attention to detail.
